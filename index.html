Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-14

English

SubTea respects your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

  • On-device processing: Text analysis is performed locally on your device using Apple Foundation Models.
  • No tracking: SubTea does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection tools.
  • Purchases: In-app purchases are processed securely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store payment information.
  • Local storage: Any user preferences are stored only on your device.

Privacy questions: privacy.subtea@proton.me

Italiano

SubTea rispetta la tua privacy. L’app non raccoglie, memorizza o trasmette dati personali.

  • Elaborazione locale: l’analisi del testo avviene esclusivamente sul dispositivo tramite Apple Foundation Models.

  • Archiviazione: le preferenze dell’utente vengono salvate solo sul dispositivo.
